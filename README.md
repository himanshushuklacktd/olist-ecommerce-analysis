# Olist Brazilian E-Commerce — Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
End-to-end data analytics project analyzing Brazilian 
e-commerce trends using the Olist dataset (2016–2018).

## Tools Used
- **SQL (MySQL)** — Data cleaning & analysis
- **Excel** — Pivot tables & summary reports  
- **Tableau** — Interactive dashboard
- **GitHub** — Version control & portfolio

## Business Questions Answered
1. What is the total revenue and order volume?
2. Which product categories drive the most revenue?
3. Which states have the highest order concentration?
4. How has revenue grown month-over-month?
5. What is the late delivery rate and its impact?

## Key Insights
- Total revenue of R$15.4M across 96,469 orders (2016–2018)
- Revenue grew 15x from 2016 to 2018
- November 2017 saw +53% MoM spike driven by Black Friday
- São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro & Minas Gerais = ~60% of orders
- 8.06% late delivery rate with avg 12.5 days delivery time
- Bed Bath Table, Health Beauty & Computers = top 3 categories

## Business Recommendations
1. Invest in Q4 seasonal campaigns — Black Friday drives 53% spike
2. Expand fulfillment centers in Southeast region (SP/RJ/MG)
3. Investigate late delivery root cause by seller location
